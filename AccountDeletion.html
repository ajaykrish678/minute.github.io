Account Deletion Request for Minute Bazaar

To request deletion of your account and associated data for Minute Bazaar, please follow these steps:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (If Applicable)

If your app has an in-app account deletion feature, you can delete your account directly within the app:

  1. Open the Minute Bazaar app on your device.
  2. Go to your profile.
  3. Go to App Settings.
  4. Find the "Delete Account" option.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your deletion request.

Data Deletion Information

When you delete your account, the following data will be deleted:

  • Profile information (name, email, etc.)
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • User-generated content (posts, uploads, etc.)

The following data may be retained for legal and accounting purposes:

  • Transaction logs: We retain transaction logs for 7 years to comply with tax regulations.
